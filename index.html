Day 2: Pamba & Sannidhanam

1. Arrival & Rapid Transfer

11:30 AM - 2:30 PM
  • Arrive Chengannur (CNGR) Time: 11:27 AM via Jan Shatabdi.
  • Hire Taxi Immediately Don't wait for buses. Book a prepaid taxi or cab from the stand outside.
    Instruction to Driver: "Direct to Pamba. No halts. Need to reach before 3 PM."
  • The Route (approx 2.5 - 3 hrs) Chengannur → Vadasserikkara → Perunad → Nilakkal → Pamba.

2. Pamba Rituals (The Foot of the Hill)

2:30 PM - 3:00 PM

Pamba Snanam

Take a holy dip in the Pamba river (Triveni Sangamam). If time is very tight, sprinkle water (Prokshanam) and wash feet.

Pamba Ganapathy

Visit the Pamba Maha Ganapathy Temple. Break coconut here if you have one in Irumudi meant for this.

MUST DO: Scan your Virtual Q Ticket at the verification counter near Pamba Manalpuram before starting the climb.

3. The Trek to Sannidhanam

3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Start: Neelimala

Steep climb. Walk slowly. Chant "Swamiye Saranam Ayyappa".

Appachimedu

Another steep section. There are worship spots for Kaduthaswamy.

Sabari Peedam

Historical spot where Sabari awaited Lord Rama. Break coconut here if required.

Sannidhanam (Arrival)

You reach the sacred Patinettam Padi (18 Steps).

4. At Sannidhanam

  1. Room Check-in: Go to Pranavam Guest House (PC-1), Room 218. Show your receipt/ID.
  2. Irumudi: Keep Irumudi safe in the room or carry it for Darshan.
  3. Darshan: Join the queue for Patinettam Padi. Your slot is 4 PM - 5 PM. Don't worry if you are slightly late; queues are continuous.
  4. Rest/Dinner: Annadhanam counters serve free food, or buy from hotels.
  5. Harivarasanam: Temple closes at night (usually 11 PM) with the Harivarasanam song. A divine experience to witness.

Day 3: Return & Temple Visits

Friday, Dec 5, 2025

Morning Rituals

Neyyabhishekam (Ghee offering) starts at 3:30 AM. If you have ghee in your Irumudi, do this early.

Checkout & Descent (8:00 AM - 11:00 AM)

Check out by 8:00 AM. Trek down takes less time (approx 1.5 hours). Reach Pamba by 10:30 - 11:00 AM.

Temples to Visit on Return

You have plenty of time before your 9:25 PM train. Negotiate with your return taxi to visit these:

1

Nilakkal Mahadeva Temple

The base camp temple. Worship Lord Shiva here.

2

Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple

A major heritage temple on the banks of Pamba. Famous for Aranmula Kannadi (Mirror). It is on the way to Chengannur.

3

Chengannur Mahadeva Temple

Located very close to the railway station. Unique temple where the deity is depicted with Devi.
